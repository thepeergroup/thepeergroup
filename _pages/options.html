As a video producer,

Matt will work with you to develop online video-based programs that replace the in-person programs your organization used to rely on for income.

For a smaller budget than you'd need with a video production studio, Matt will guide you through all the stages of program development, including ideation, planning, filming, editing, and marketing.

Matt's most recent work involved creating an online program for a retreat center that cannot presently offer in-person retreats. The program, a 3-hour course in making plant-based skin care remedies, evoked the presenters' genuine personalities and the knowledge they wanted to share, as well as a sense of place.
